Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What subjects do you tutor?

v

We offer tutoring in Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, and more — for elementary, high school, and college students.

Is your service only online?

v

No. We provide both in-person and online sessions. We also accommodate students without consistent access to internet or electricity.

Who can join Top Academy?

v

Any motivated student, teacher, or graduate can apply — and we also partner with schools, NGOs, and media platforms.

Can parents track student progress?

v

Yes! We have strong parent-teacher collaboration, including regular updates and shared reports.

How much do your services cost?

v

Our pricing varies based on the service, level, and frequency. We strive to keep our services affordable and offer scholarships for qualifying students. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer group sessions?

v

Yes, we offer both one-on-one and group tutoring sessions. Group sessions are a cost-effective option that still provides personalized attention in a collaborative environment.
